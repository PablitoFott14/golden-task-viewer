## Agent Objective

Rebeca Davis is a QA Engineer at Nexbridge Technologies and needs to send her manager, Colton, a report on the latest status of the refund module. The deadline is approaching, so she needs the agent help to determine the final state of each bug using her personal Jira logs and the RC screenshots. As part of that review, she also wants the agent to create a corresponding ticket for each unresolved issue, following the company’s latest ticket format present at email, and send a Slack message to her teammates, Amara and Kai.

No bugs other than the ones recorded in the Jira logs should be addressed.

## Desired Outcome

The agent cross-references each of the 11 bugs in Rebecca's personal (stale) Jira notes against the matching RC screenshot to determine its true current status (7 resolved, 4 unresolved), rather than trusting the "TO DO" board at face value. It generates `refund_mod_QA_results.svg` following `svg_format.txt` exactly, labels each unresolved issue with its correct company issue key (2 recovered from the team's tracker email, the remaining 9 generated by continuing the sequence from the latest registered key), appends a `SOLVED`/`UNSOLVED` breakdown to `MEMORY.md`, posts a matching summary to `#qa-team` asking Kai and Amara to follow up on the unresolved bugs, and emails the SVG to Colton with the exact requested subject line. No bug outside Rebecca's own Jira notes is addressed. See `GTFA.md` for the full ground truth.
